PROBLEM: This bank loses ~20% of customers annually (2,037 of 10,000).
Average customer lifetime value ≈ €5,000 (estimate using Balance + tenure).
Cost of a retention offer (discount/relationship manager call) ≈ €150.

GOAL: Build a system that ranks customers by churn risk so the bank
can proactively target the top 15% highest-risk customers with
retention offers — instead of guessing or reacting after they leave.
